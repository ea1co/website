Our approach

We build our own toolstack, embedded with the ways we see the world.

After more than a decade of studying how communities actually form — how people gather, participate, and influence each other — we developed a set of principles about how that works. The EA1 Way shapes every strategy we write and every recommendation we make. It made sense to encode those same principles into the instruments we use every day.

Each tool here is built around a specific need from the work itself: listening to what communities are actually saying, understanding the diversity of motivations within an audience, checking whether our own output aligns with how we think. They are designed for population thinking, not persona averaging. For context, not just content.

We build them iteratively, in conversation with AI, shaped by editorial judgment. A new feature can ship the same day someone asks for it. The tools stay close to the work because they grow directly out of it.
