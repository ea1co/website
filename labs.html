How we build

We got tired of paying for dashboards nobody opened.

Enterprise tools try to serve everyone, which means they serve no one particularly well. The learning curves are steep, the interfaces are generic, and the insights require a PhD in filtering to find.

So we started building our own. Each tool is purpose-built for one job, shaped to how our team actually works, and iterable in real time. When someone needs a new feature, it ships the same day.

We call this approach vibe-coded tooling — built in conversation with AI, grounded in editorial judgment, and designed to encode The EA1 Way into software the team can use every day.

What's next

From internal tools to shared capabilities.

Every tool we build encodes a piece of how EA1 thinks — our editorial judgment, our community analysis frameworks, our strategy methodology. Right now these tools are internal. But the architecture is simple enough that they can become services.

If you're a client or a partner who wants access to a specific capability — say, a Reddit landscape scan for your quarterly review, or audience insights for a campaign — we can make that happen. Not as a SaaS product, but as a capability you can tap into. Per-project, per-engagement.
